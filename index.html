NetMedia AI Lab @ Inner Mongolia University

NetMedia AI Lab was established in 2014 by Ma Ming, a teacher at the School of Computer (Software) of Inner Mongolia University. Based on academic research and applied research, combined with the real needs of regions and enterprises in information construction, guided by exploring scientific problems faced in the field of computer science, the team adheres to the guiding ideology of "research strives for originality, technological breakthroughs, and achievements promote industry", and forms characteristics and advantages in three aspects: scientific research, high-level talent training and enterprise application.
